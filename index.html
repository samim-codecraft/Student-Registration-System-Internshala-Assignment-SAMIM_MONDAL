Student Registration System

Student Registration System is developed with an objective that management of student information shall be made as streamlined as possible. The system has increased the user-friendly registration process with respect to information like names, classes, address, and contact number of students. There is a provision for editing and deleting the existing records so that data will be an error-free and latest one. Simple user interface makes the procedure simple and facilitates easy control over the handling of student information in general.
